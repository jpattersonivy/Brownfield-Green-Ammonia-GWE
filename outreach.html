Global Outreach & Development Sites

Green ammonia is already being deployed worldwide. Norway leads with a successful brownfield retrofit, while Sri Lanka, Kenya, and Puerto Rico stand out as promising pilot development sites.

Norway — Herøya Industrial Park

Norway operates one of the world’s first brownfield green ammonia retrofits.

Sri Lanka — Emerging Pilot Region

Sri Lanka’s strong solar potential makes it ideal for renewable-powered ammonia production.

Kenya — East African Development Hub

Kenya’s agricultural demand and industrial growth make it a prime candidate for green ammonia.

Puerto Rico — Caribbean Pilot Site

Puerto Rico’s grid instability highlights the need for resilient ammonia-powered microgrids.

Global Adoption Timeline
